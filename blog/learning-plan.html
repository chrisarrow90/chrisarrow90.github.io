Cultural Blog - My Learning Plan

30 June 2021

Foundations Learning Plan

What is my long term goal and/or Career Pathway?

Explore a future career shift into the IT industry, either into a Full-Stack web development role or into software development.

What are my strengths and limitations in relation to learning?

Strengths - highly motivated, fast learner

Weaknesses - staying focussed and not letting attention divert to something else

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would I like to see developed in myself while at EDA?

I would really like to work on my collaborative skills and gain more confidence in public speaking and presentations.

How will I manage my workload during this programme?

  • I will make Weekly/Daily plans to keep track of what I need to do
  • I will utilise Toggle to keep track of time spent on activities
  • I will work in an environment free from distractions
  • I will take regular breaks to keep a fresh mind and maximise my producitivity
  • I will work with my Cohort and Facilitators to maintain accountability of one another
  • If I am feeling overwhelemed, I will take a break and do some exercise, go for a walk and clear my head. If I am stuck on a problem, I will reach out to others for support and guidance

How and where will I seek help in a timely way?

I will use Slack as the platform to communicate with other students and the faciliation team

What I expect from the facilitation team?

I expect them to guide me in my learning rather than just give me the answers to my questions in order to help me grow as a developer. I expect to receive constructive criticism in both my technical and soft skills with advice on how to improve

What scheduling conflicts do I have and how will I plan around these?

At this point I do not have any real scheduling conflicts, just daily gym routine that I will organise around the course

Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is my long term goal and/or career pathway?

My goal is still to seek a full stack web development job. I have a stronger preference towards working on backend as I believe that is were my real strengths are.

What will my biggest strengths and limitations in Bootcamp be?

I believe my organised nature, high levels of motivation and flexibility will be my biggest strengths. My biggest limitations will be overcoming my introverted personality..

What will be my biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp?

I think it will be split between two areas: keeping focus for the full 10 weeks, and also public speaking in front of a group audience especially if it has to do with personal reflection

What non-technical skills (human skills) would I like to see developed in myself while at EDA?

I would like to develop my collaboration and pair programming skills, public speaking and also develop my leadership capability.

What are my expectations from the Bootcamp team?

To assist in a helpful manner and help me grow. To receive open and honest feedback and advice.

What are my expectations of myself on Bootcamp?

My expectations are to maintain focus, stay on top of the workload, and to give 100% towards the course. I will co-operate with and interact with the other members of my cohort in a helpful and friendly manner and help each other maintain accountability.
