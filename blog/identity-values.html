My Core Values

  • Honesty and Integrity
  • Respect
  • Kindness

My Strengths

  • Organised — Planned, organised and structured in my approach, I am responsible and process driven.
  • Inquisitive — My inquisitive nature makes me a flexible problem solver striking a good balance between thinking and doing.
  • Motivated — I have strong determination and am results-oriented.
  • Versatile — I thrive on diversity and unpredictability.
  • Relaxed — My ability to remain calm under pressure makes me great in crisis situations or working to tight deadlines.

My Weaknesses

  • Focus — My inquisitive nature can lead me to being unable to stay focused on learning a single topic with a preference of pratice over theory.
  • Reserved — Being introverted can make establishing new relationships challenging and coming across initially as quite reserved.
  • Leadership — I tend to shy away from leadership positions.
  • Critical — I hold myself to high standard and can be quite hard on myself if I don't perform as well as I had hoped.
